About Me

Getting things right is the whole point.

I got into data because I'm obsessed with accuracy. Not roughly right, actually right. That fits perfectly with my current role at dnata Catering UK, where if I record a seal number wrong or miss a discrepancy on a loading list, that flight doesn't go. I like the moment where messy data becomes a clear answer, and I'm equally comfortable writing the queries as presenting the findings to stakeholders.

I'm based in London, holding CompTIA Data+, Google Data Analytics, and BCS Foundation in Business Analysis certifications, and actively looking for a Junior Data Analyst or Data Quality role.
